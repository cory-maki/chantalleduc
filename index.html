About Chantal

A Life Shaped by Style, Culture & Timeless Elegance

Chantal Leduc is a French-Canadian fashion stylist, designer, and former international model whose life and career have been shaped by a deep appreciation for beauty, culture, and timeless elegance. Born and raised in Montreal, Canada, she began her journey in fashion at a young age, embarking on an international modeling career that took her across continents.

Now focused on fashion styling and design, Chantal combines her international experience with an innate sense of refinement and individuality. A regular presence at Women's Fashion Week in Milan and Paris, she draws inspiration from the world's most celebrated fashion houses and evolving artistic movements.

Beyond couture, Chantal's passions extend to travel and architecture — she is captivated by beautifully designed spaces, historic residences, and grand European hotels. Above all, she considers her four accomplished adult children her greatest pride.
