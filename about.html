The Early Years

From Montreal to the International Runway

Chantal Leduc's story begins in Montreal, Canada, where she was born and raised. It was there that her lifelong love of fashion first took root, and where she took her earliest steps toward a career in the industry.

That early passion led to a successful international modeling career that took her across continents, introducing her to some of fashion's most inspiring creative influences along the way. Those formative years shaped both her aesthetic sensibility and the work she pursues today.

Style & Design

A Refined Eye for Timeless Style

Today, Chantal is focused on fashion styling and design, combining her international modeling experience with an innate sense of refinement and individuality. Her aesthetic is informed by both classic sophistication and contemporary luxury.

The result is a style that feels effortlessly elegant and deeply personal — one that Chantal Leduc brings to every project, whether she's styling an individual client or shaping the creative direction of a broader collaboration.

Paris & Milan

A Regular Presence at Fashion Week

Chantal is a familiar face at Women's Fashion Week in both Milan and Paris, where she draws inspiration from the world's most celebrated fashion houses, emerging designers, and evolving artistic movements.

Her travels keep her connected to the pulse of global fashion while continually enriching her creative vision — an ongoing dialogue between what she sees on the runway and what she brings back to her own work.

Travel & Architecture

Finding Inspiration in Design and Place

Beyond the world of couture, Chantal's passions extend to travel and architecture. She is captivated by beautifully designed spaces, historic residences, grand European hotels, and the architectural details that tell the story of a place.

Whether strolling through the streets of Paris, discovering hidden gems in Milan, or exploring destinations further afield, Chantal Leduc finds inspiration in the intersection of design, culture, and history.

Family First

Her Greatest Pride

Above all, Chantal's greatest pride is her family. The mother of four accomplished adult children, she considers them her most meaningful achievement and greatest source of joy.

Their accomplishments, character, and close bond remain at the center of her life. Today, Chantal embodies a lifestyle defined by elegance, creativity, and curiosity — one she continues to share through her work and travels.
